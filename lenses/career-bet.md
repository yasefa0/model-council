# Career Bet Framework

- **Signal vs. Noise**: What's real traction vs. wishful thinking?
- **Optionality**: Does this open or close future doors?
- **Fit Gap**: Where is the genuine skill/experience delta?
- **Asymmetry**: What's the upside if it works vs. downside if it doesn't?
